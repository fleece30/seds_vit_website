Think Infinite

CORE

The core committee is responsible for conducting various space-themed conferences, events, and competitions within our campus for students from colleges all over the country. It also collaborates with SEDS India for national-level events which allow the participants to see the space industry as an exciting and viable career option.

OUTREACH

Our Outreach programme extends to the local schools in and around Vellore where we teach the students about how the universe works using interactive teaching methods and an in-depth curriculum. The programme helps us stay true to our mission of arousing interest in space exploration among young students from all backgrounds and empowering them to pursue it further.

PROJECTS

The Projects division meanwhile focuses on the technical aspects of space exploration by working on individual, or team-projects such as Mars rovers and UAVs for international competitions. Members are encouraged to use their academic learning to pursue their passion and help boost India’s bid to become a space-superpower.

The Star Party

Star Party is an annual stargazing event which is held by SEDS VIT every year during GraVITas. It focuses on the observational astronomy whereby different celestial and deep sky objects are viewed through the telescopes and binoculars by the participants. The histories, myths, the scientific reasoning and facts of these deep sky objects are also discussed during the stargazing session.

The OneSpace Initiative by SEDS India lives by the motto of educating the youth about the wonders of astronomy and space science. ASTRA

A first-of-its-kind satellite design based hackathon of India – Astra is conducted by SEDS-VIT. Astra is organized with the sole purpose of keeping up with changing trends and driving innovation in the field of space exploration. The hack consists of three rounds, where teams from all over India and abroad, were given problem statements based on real-life challenges with a multifaceted judging panel brimming with experience in engineering and astronomy to mentor and evaluate the participants.

India's first Satellite Design Hackathon
